How to play

  • Click a card (or pile top) to select it, then click a destination pile to move.
  • Double-click a card to auto-move it to the first valid foundation (ace deck).
  • Drag and drop a face-up card (and any valid sequence under it) onto a valid destination pile.
  • Use Undo to revert the most recent draw or card move.
  • In Klondike/Patience, click the stock pile to draw cards.
  • In FreeCell, use free cells to temporarily store cards.
